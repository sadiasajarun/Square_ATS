How this report works — and what it will never do
  • Publicly available professional information only. Assistive research to support HR verification — never a screening input, never part of the AI match score, never an automated decision.
  • Identity is confirmed once, by a human, on the profile photo and name. AI only proposes matches; nothing below the identity step is used until a person confirms it.
  • Personal, social, and protected-characteristic information is excluded by design. The photo is a recognition aid only — never analysed, never scored.
  • Every lookup is logged to the audit trail. Requires STL Legal/HR approval and is opt-in per position.
  • Absence of evidence is not evidence. A low online-presence score usually just means a private person and is never a reason to reject.
Excluded by design. This report deliberately does not retrieve or score: personal social media content, photos as data, religion, political views, marital or family status, health, or any protected characteristic. Where a public social account appears below, only professional public activity is summarised.
