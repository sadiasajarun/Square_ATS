How this tool works — and what it will never do
  • Publicly available professional information only. Assistive research to support HR verification — not a screening input, never an automated decision.
  • Identity is confirmed once, by you, on the profile photo and name. AI only proposes a match; nothing is used until you confirm it.
  • Personal, social, and protected-characteristic information is excluded by design. Photos are a recognition aid only — never analyzed, never scored.
  • Every lookup is logged in the audit trail. Requires STL Legal/HR approval and is opt-in per position.
